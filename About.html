About the Tour:

.
One Day Adventure Package with Transportation:
Enjoy a full day of touring Arenal. You will see the beauty of the La Fortuna Waterfall and you will hike down to the cool waters below; a delicious lunch at a local restaurant will follow. After lunch, you will head to the Hanging Bridges for some more hiking and you will observe the flora and fauna of the area. There will be a guided hike near the Arenal Volcano and from there you will proceed to the Baldi Hot Springs for a buffet dinner and a soak in the thermal hot springs.

Departure Time: 8:00 am
Return Time: 9 pm
Includes: All transportation from Hotel, all entrance fees, a bilingual guide, refreshments, lunch and dinner.
Price: $160


Arenal Volcano Hiking Tour with Transportation:
This tour takes you to the active side of Arenal! I will take you through rainforest trails for a pleasent and calming hike; you can view the diversity of the wildlife and plants (such as monkeys,toucans, wild turkeys, sloths, a large variety of birds, and sometimes snakes!). While observing the volcano and taking pictures, I will share the history of the area as well as more information about the volcano's activity. You will then continue down to the exotic and relaxing hot springs nearby.
*Baldi Hot Springs is the only option with this tour.

Departure Time: 3:00 pm
Returning Time: 9:30 pm
Includes: Transportation , a bilingual guide, park entrance, hot springs entrance and buffet dinner.
Price: $78
