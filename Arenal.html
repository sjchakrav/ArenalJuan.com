    A Map of Arenal:



    Costa Rica is a beautiful Country in Central America and is mostly considered a rainforest. As such, there is a huge population of Flora and Fauna to observe and learn from. In addition to this, there is an abundance of wildlife that maintains the ecosystem. You will be touring Arenal, which is home to the famous Arenal Volcano. This majestic creation of nature stands at 5,358 ft and recently erupted in 1968 after many years of dormancy.
